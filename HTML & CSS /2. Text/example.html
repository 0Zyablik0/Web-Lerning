The story of the Book

Chapter 1

Molly has been staring out of her window for about one hour now. On her desk, lying between the copies of Nature, New Scientist, and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in , was the well thumbed copy of On The Road. It had been Molly's favorite book since college, and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in this room, sitting under the poster with Oscar Wilde quote proclaiming that Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do . Although many considered her pioneering work, unraveling the secretes of the llama DNA, to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
